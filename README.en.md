## Business Problem
The company needs to understand which international markets generate real profitability after marketing investments.

## Objective
Evaluate revenue, costs and marketing ROI by country to guide budget allocation decisions.

## Dataset
Aggregated financial performance by country including:
- Revenue
- Costs
- Campaign Cost
- Gross Profit
- Margin %
- ROI %

## Methodology
Data was prepared using SQL transformations and analyzed through comparative financial analysis using the CFI framework (Context, Findings, Implications).

## Key Insights
- United States shows significantly higher ROI (75.75%) compared to France and Canada (~18%)
- Marketing costs in low-performing markets reduce profitability
- Positive ROI does not guarantee real business profitability

## Business Recommendations
- Gradually reduce marketing spend in low ROI markets
- Complement campaign ROI with full operational cost metrics

SQL | Spreadsheet Analysis | Business Analytics
